Scheduling App

Offering you the ability to set up scheduled appointments faster and easier than ever before. This application is desigend to allow you - the admin - to setup predetermined available dates and times for scheduled meetings. Then, simply email a URL to your clients and let them take care of the time/date, as well as modifying dates/times for interviews. No-longer will you need to communicate back and forth with your clients to confirm a date and time for a interview/meeting/appointment. Save time, and use SchedulingApp!
